Magnolia flowers are edible flowers that bloom into either a white, pink, yellow, green, or purple hue. They can take on a star shape or bowl shape. Only use the flower petals when cooking with magnolia flowers. Magnolia flowers can be used as a raw garnish on a salad, infused into a syrup, and even pickled. Their stunning beauty is perfect to adorn a wedding cake, especially if the flower is in the main bouquet.


What Does Magnolia Taste Like?

Ginger, sharp cardamom notes


Magnolia Flower Uses:

Salad garnish, dessert garnish, cake decorations, pickled, infused in syrup
